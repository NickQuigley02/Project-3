Range of the Pygmy Blue Whale, or Balaenoptera musculus brevicauda.


The Pygmy Blue Whale is a subspecies of Blue Whale. It reaches a size of "only" 79 feet, hence the common name. It is one of the most numerous of all Blue Whale species, with a population estimated to be around 5000, or the equivalent of 94 NFL rosters.
